### Notes
This is one of the first strains I isolated from Wisconsin. It made me realize the media we normally use (HEPES-WC pH 7.8) is not sufficient to grow a multitide of cultures and we are artificially selecting for individuals that are able to survive in our selective culture conditions. Most of these strains only grow in MES-Volvox pH 6.8 unless noted otherwise.
