### Notes
AMD-EK1, AMD-EK2, and AMD-EK3 may all be the same species and the appear to be most similar to other Coleochaete pulvinata or Coleochaete soluta. The are almost certainly neither and may be separate species from one another. Further DNA sequencing will be needed to confirm this.
