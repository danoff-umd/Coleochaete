### Notes
This was by far the hardest species to isolate and appears most similar to Coleochaete conchaeta. This was isolated not as a thallus but as a single zoospore found during thallus isolation on a piece of birch bark. The thalli growing on the birch bark were the most abundant species in lake Erickson and were shapped like little mounds. Currently I am not able to grow it in liquid culture but it was adapted fine to solid agar media
