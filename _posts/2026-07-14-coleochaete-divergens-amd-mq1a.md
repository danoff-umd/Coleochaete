### Notes
This strain was the first to be found in Monacqua right by the shore on a stick. It was quite difficult to tell it was Coleochaete at first as it was just a mass of cells. It has sort of zig-zag cell division and when the thalli are large they appear like Coleochaete pulvintata although this is almost certainly more closely related to Coleochaete divergens.
