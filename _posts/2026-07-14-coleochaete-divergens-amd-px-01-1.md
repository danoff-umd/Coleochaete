### Notes
This strain was isolated at the same time as AMD-PX-06-1 however this organisms are very likely different species. If taken care of well and the media is changed at least once a week. The culture will being to sexually reproduce and zygotes will form in around 3 weeks to a month. Forms many more branches than AMD-PX-06-1
