### Notes
This strain was found growing overtop a the isolated strain of Coleochaete scutata from Australia. When the thalli are young they resemble Coleochaete divergens/nitellarum but as they grow older they more resemble Coleochaete conchaeta/pulvinata. Antheridia have been observed on older thalli but no zygotes have ever formed. Likely dioecious.
